OurCourses

At our photography school, we offer a variety of
courses that cover all aspects of this fascinating art.
Regardless of your experience or skill level, we have
courses to suit your needs and goals.

      01

      Basics of
      photography

      During this course, you will be able to
      familiarize yourself with different types of
      cameras, learn the basics of composition.

      02

      Landscape
      photography

      In this course, you'll learn how to choose the
      right angles, frame landscapes, and create
      depth and perspective in your images.

      03

      Portrait
      photography

      During this course, you will learn to choose
      the right lighting, reproduce the mood and
      expressions on the faces of the models.
